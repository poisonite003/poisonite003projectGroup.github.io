About VOrgreens

VOrgreens is an Indonesia’s first plant based eatery chain serving delicious healthy, ethical and sustainable food. VOrgreens provides many variant cuisines that are undoubtedly healthy and delicious.

Our Vision

“Build a healthy and nutritious food life for the body.”

Our Mission

  1. Helping fruit and vegetable farmers to increase their income.
  2. Helping many people to start a healthy life by consuming fruits, vegetables, and meats that have been measured for their nutritional content.
  3. Serving healthy food with the best quality directly from nature

Our History

After seeing the development of food at this time, a lot of food is made, but do not pay attention to the quality and content such as vitamins and minerals that are very important for the body. Therefore, our restaurant creates a food that is equipped with nutritional needs so that the needs in the body can be fulfilled properly. Thus, helping the body's performance in order to maximize the activities that will be carried out daily.
